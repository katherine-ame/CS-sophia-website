Croissant

The first baked good I want to talk about is croissants! The modern croissant orginated in France, but it is now enjoyed all over the world. A croissant is a savory, flaky pastry often made of butter, flour, sugar, salt, yeast, and milk. There are many different toppings that you can put in them, such as chocolate, cream, fruits, or different types of nuts.

Here is a recipe you could use to make croisants at home. Be warned: it can take at least a day to make them! Link to recipe: https://baranbakery.com/easy-croissant-recipe/.
